Writing Samples

Midas Touch

Midas lives in poverty. He is also an alchemist and, like every alchemist before him, Midas longs to learn how to create gold. One late work-filled night becomes life changing when he finally figures out the correct formula. But infinite gold isn't all it seems and life changing doesn't have to mean good.

Coccoon

Sam has never left her small town before and she is more than a little worried about her new life in the big city. Those worries are made only worse by the strange insect hive in her new apartment and the unsettling man she keeps seeing aroud the city. But it's probably fine! Right?
